A full day rafting trip on the Colorado River includes 6 hours of time on the water, a hot lunch, all necessary equipment, safety spotters, and transportation to and from the river. This was a Class 3 trip on the Colorado River!

A half day rafting trip includes 3 hours on the water, all necessary equipment, safety spotters, and transportation to and from the river. This is a Class 2 section of the Colorado River.

Membership in the Razor's Edge Extreme Team requires you to pass a competency exam. Once you are a member, you can participate in Class 5 and above trips. These trips include the same amenities as lower class trips, but all guides are certified in high risk water rescue and emergency first aid.
