Razor's Edge Rafting Company

If you want to experience the thrills of white-water rafting, Razor's Edge Rafting Company is dedicated to providing you with the best experience. Our staff is dedicated to providing you with the right amount of thrill for you while ensuring your safe return!

History

Joseph and Purity Bowen have been residents of Colorado since 2001. They currently reside in Commerce City where they are raising their three children (Serenity, Naomi, and Devon). They are heavily involved in their community through Karate. All but Devon have achieved their black belts. As the youngest, Devon will be achieving his black belt soon. They bring the Black Belt values of dedication, service, and fun to everything they do.

In 2010, the Bowen family embarked on the journey of providing safe and engaging entertainment to the community by starting Razor's Edge Rafting Company. They started by providing white-water rafting trips on Clear Creek in the mountains of Colorado. They have since grown to offer trips on the Colorado River, the Eagle River, and the Blue River. Whichever river you choose, Razor's Edge has the perfect rafting trip for you!
