Joseph's Favorite City

Bozeman Montana

Bozeman, Montana is my Favorite city because I grew up in the area. It is nestled in the Gallatin Valley of the beautiful Rocky mountains. Bozeman has easy access to hiking, skiing, four-wheeling, camping, and all kinds of outdoor activities. It has enough people to provide needed services, but not so many people that it is overcrowded. Bozeman is home to Montana State University. Bozeman is also the home to Rosa's Pizza which has by far the best pizza I have ever tasted! Bozeman offers the ability to live a country lifestyle while enjoying all of the necessary city comforts.
